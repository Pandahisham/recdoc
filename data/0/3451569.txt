A case of spontaneous intracranial hypotensionwith typical magnetic resonance images

Spontaneous intracranialhypotension (SIH) is a rare syndromedefined by posturalheadache, associated with a lowcerebrospinal fluid pressure, withouthistory of previous dural traumaor invasive treatment on rachis. Wereported a case of a patient withpostural headache caused by SIHidentified by magnetic resonanceimages and treated with saline solutioninfusion with complete remissionof symptoms.
