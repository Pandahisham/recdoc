Erratum


The publisher and authors would like to draw the reader's attention to an error in the following article.
Diffusion and perfusion characteristics of MELAS (mitochondrial myopathy, encephalopathy, lactic acidosis, and stroke-like episode) in thirteen patients. Korean J Radiol 2011;12:15-24.
On page 15, the 4th author's name has been incorrectly spelled as Jung Ho Rha. The correct spelling is Joung Ho Rha.
On page 15, the last author's name has been incorrectly spelled as Chang Hae Shu. The correct spelling is Chang Hae Suh.
