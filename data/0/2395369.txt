Metastatic Liposarcoma: A Cause of Symptomatic Acute Pericarditis

This is an open access article distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution License, which permits unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited.
We describe a patient presenting with a myxoid liposarcoma of the lower thigh in whom an episode of acute pericarditisindicated the recurrence of widespread metastatic disease.
