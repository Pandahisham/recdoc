Erratum


The publisher and authors would like to draw the reader's attention to an error in the following article.
MR Imaging of the Spine at 3.0T with T2-Weighted IDEAL Fast Recovery Fast Spin-Echo Technique. Korean J Radiol 2012;13:44-52.
On page 1, the country's name of all authors has been incorrectly spelled as Republic of China. The correct spelling is People's Republic of China.
