A Case of RhabdomyoSarcoma Following a Metal Surgical Implant

This is an open access article distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution License, which permits unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited.
Patient. We report a 51-year-old male presenting with Grade            III rhabdomyosarcoma.
Discussion. A case of rhabdomyosarcoma which developed in            proximity to a metal surgical implant is described. Few cases have been reported in            the world in humans.The therapeutic approach to the disease is presented, together            with a brief review of literature.
