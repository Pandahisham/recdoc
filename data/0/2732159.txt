Rother KI, Brown RJ, Morales MM, Wright E, Duan Z, Campbell C, Harlan DM, Orlander PR, Brod SA, Hardin DS, Popovic J, McEvoy RC. Effect of ingested interferon-α on β-cell function in children with new-onset type 1 diabetes. Diabetes Care 2009;32:1250–1255
In the print version of the article listed above, the author sequence is incorrect. The correct author sequence is as follows: Rother KI, Brown RJ, Morales MM, Wright E, Duan Z, Campbell C, Hardin DS, Popovic J, McEvoy RC, Harlan DM, Orlander PR, Brod SA. The online version reflects these changes.
