Errata


Harb G, Vasavada RC, Cobrinik D, Stewart AF. The retinoblastoma protein and its homolog p130 regulate the G1/S transition in pancreatic β-cells. Diabetes 2009;58:1852–1862
In the Acknowledgments section in the print version of the article listed above, the acronym JDRF has been incorrectly expanded as Junior Diabetes Research Foundation. The correct expansion of the acronym JDRF is Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation. The online version reflects these changes.
