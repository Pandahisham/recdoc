


Expression of B lymphocyte chemoattractant (BLC) in splenic follicles of wild-type mice. The BLC in situ hybridization signal is seen as darker purple staining. Expression of BLC is markedly reduced in spleens of mice lacking TNF, TNFR1, or lymphotoxin α/β,  suggesting a mechanism for the disruption in lymphocyte compartmentalization in these animals. See related article in this issue by Ngo et al.,  pp. 403–412.
