Valsalva-induced cluster headache

A case valsalva-inducedcluster headache is presented.Cluster attacks induced by valsalvamanoeuvres alone were onlyrecently described, and suchpatients have features of coughheadache and cluster headache.Attacks occurred a couple oftimes a week in the patientpresented, solely triggered by valsalvamanoeuvres includingcoughing, sneezing or strainingand not by exercising.
